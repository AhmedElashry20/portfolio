I am a Senior Flutter Developer and Full-Stack Engineer with over 8 years of professional experience designing, building, and shipping high-quality mobile and web applications. My work spans Fintech, Logistics, E-commerce, and Enterprise Resource Planning, with 38+ production apps serving more than 100,000 active users worldwide.

My career journey includes Domina Coral Bay (2018–2020), international experience in Italy (2020–2022), Gomla & Bas (2022–2023), HelpTK (2023–2024), Sumo Software (2024–2025), and now Bin Rabie Holding Group in Riyadh where I lead mobile and web development initiatives, architect scalable backend systems with Node.js and PostgreSQL, and manage IT infrastructure across multiple subsidiaries.

I hold 30 professional certifications from Google, AWS, Meta, Stanford, Harvard, and more. I am passionate about clean architecture, continuous learning, and leveraging AI to push the boundaries of what software can achieve.
